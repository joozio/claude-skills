# Content Creation Skill

Use this skill when creating written content for various platforms.

## When to Use

- Writing blog posts for your publication
- Creating social media posts (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Threads)
- Drafting newsletters
- Writing documentation or guides
- Creating marketing copy

## Content Types

### Blog Posts

**Style:** Personal, insightful, actionable
**Length:** 800-1500 words

Structure:
```markdown
# [Catchy Title]

[Hook - 1-2 sentences that grab attention]

## The Problem/Context
[Set up why this matters]

## The Insight/Solution
[Main content - your unique perspective]

## Practical Application
[How to use this]

## Takeaway
[One clear action item]

---
*[Personal sign-off]*
```

### X/Twitter Posts

**Character limit:** 280 (or thread for longer)
**Style:** Punchy, valuable, conversational

Formats that work:
- Insight + one-liner
- "Here's what I learned..." thread
- Controversial take + explanation
- Quick tip with example

Thread structure:
- Tweet 1: Hook (make them read on)
- Tweets 2-4: Core points
- Last tweet: CTA + link

### LinkedIn Posts

**Style:** Professional but human
**Length:** 150-400 words
**Structure:** Hook -> Story -> Insight -> CTA

### Newsletter

Same as blog posts but more personal, conversational tone.
Always include a subject line suggestion.

## Writing Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- First person
- Confident but not arrogant
- Practical and actionable
- Uses analogies and examples
- Avoids jargon unless necessary

### Structure Rules
1. **Lead with value** — Don't bury the insight
2. **Use short paragraphs** — 2-3 sentences max
3. **Include examples** — Concrete beats abstract
4. **End with action** — What should the reader do?

### Things to Avoid
- Em-dashes (use commas or periods instead)
- Generic AI-sounding phrases ("In today's fast-paced world...")
- Passive voice
- Burying the lede
- Corporate speak

### SEO (for blog)
- Include target keyword in title
- Use headers (H2, H3)
- Add internal/external links
- Meta description: 150-160 chars

## Quality Checklist

Before finalizing:
- [ ] Clear main point?
- [ ] Engaging opening?
- [ ] Actionable takeaway?
- [ ] No typos/grammar issues?
- [ ] Appropriate length?
- [ ] Platform-appropriate format?
- [ ] Personal and specific (not generic)?

## Output Formats

**Blog post:** Full article ready to paste
**X thread:** Tweet 1 + numbered follow-ups
**LinkedIn:** Single post (150-400 words)
**Newsletter:** Subject line + full body
